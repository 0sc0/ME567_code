Foundings:
1)DFS is not a good method. Because its path is twisty, and easy to over come the goal.
2)BFS is better than DFS, at least it's not that twisty. But sometime BFS's interation is longer,
so it is not fast enough.
3)Greedy-first method is much quicker than BFS in many scenes. But sometimes, the path is not
shortest, for example, "misc" scene.
4)A-star method is the best method in this four methods. A-star method combines the advantages of
BFS and Greedy-first method, so it can get shortest path in a quick speed.
